Mission

The mission of this project is to attract young people to join the Diggers Beach Volleyball and to improve the the number of member. This site is made because if people know about the Diggers Beach Volleyball more people will come and play with the Diggers Beach Volleyball.

Success Evaluation

Success is achieved if there's 15 people sign up and register their team to the Diggers Beach Volleyball and really come to play.

Target Audience

The target audience of the website is young kids, teenagers and young adults to play with Diggers Beach Volleyball. To attract the target audience, the design will use bright color and some pictures of beach volleyball game.
